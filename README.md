## What is Social Jailbreak?

Social media platforms have become "social jails" where users are captive to retention mechanisms and restrictive publishing interfaces. Social Jailbreak is a browser extension and app that:

1. **Intercepts your publications** with a rich text editor (like Grammarly for freedom)
2. **Stores the original** in a decentralized network (IPFS + ActivityPub)
3. **Adapts and publishes** to commercial platforms automatically
4. **Lets you consult** an aggregated, algorithm-free feed from all your networks

## Why It Matters

Migration from social media monopolies fails because it's a **collective decision**, not individual. Social Jailbreak solves this by letting you stay where your friends are while building the free web in parallel.
